Each file M3A8mxxa5_yyms.dat contains the following information at yy time 
for model with initial BH mass of 3 solar mass, initial BH spin parameter 0.8,
initial torus mass of 0.xx solar mass, and the viscosity parameter 0.05.

1. radius (cm)
2. polar angle theta (rad)
3. mass density (g/cm^3)
4. nue number density (1/cm^3)
5. nuebar number density (1/cm^3)
6. nue number flux at r direction (1/cm^2/s)
7. nue number flux at theta direction (1/cm^2/s)
8. nuebar number flux at r direction (1/cm^2/s)
9. nuebar number flux at theta direction (1/cm^2/s)
10. temperature (MeV)
11. electron chemical potential (MeV)
12. electron fraction Ye (1)
